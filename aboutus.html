About us


Vivek Chauhan -Founder and M.D.

Learn Well Institute was created in 1992 by the vision and toil of Mr. Vivek Chauhan, a Computer Engineering Graduate from Chandigarh university. We had a very humble beginning as a forum for learning computer sciences, with a vision to provide an ideal launch pad for serious software engineering aspirants.Along the way to realization of this vision, we went beyond merely coaching for computer based languages.

Today, aiming for the unachievable and continuously raising the bar has become a part of the LEARN WELL DNA .
